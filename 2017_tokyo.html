This event took place at Tokyo the 27 and 28 july 2017.

Location: Tokyo

Two hack-/concept-days for Bitcoin Core and Lightning developers, hosted by Digital Garage.
One day in Ebisu (Center of Tokyo), the other at Kamakura, near the beach. (Tourist spot, for those interested wandering the 29 july)
Twelves bed are available in 5 or 6 rooms made available only the Friday night at Kamakura. (In front of the beach)
The event takes part between two events NCC and Blockchain Core Bootcamp 2, attempted by some core developers.

Invitation required. Registration mandatory. If you are a contributor and wish to come, ping NicolasDorier.
