FAQ

Why are there no presentations?

The main purpose of the event is for active Bitcoin Core contributors to meet and accelerate completion of many outstanding projects. Meeting in person to code together is very productive. This is a "hackathon" rather than a conference which focuses on tangible work.

Why is the event "invite only"?

The "invite only" strategy aims to reduce developer distraction and allows to focus on the main goal: specifically work together on Bitcoin Core and Lightning. For this reason, only regular active contributors to Bitcoin Core and lightning implementations have been invited.

Can I participate remotely?

There won't be any video or audio streaming but developers will continue to use #bitcoin-core-dev IRC chat as normal.
